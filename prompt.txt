## Product Grades
- **NEW (Sealed):** Factory sealed, never opened or used. Manufacturer warranty **3 years** from online registration date or invoice date.
- **Grade A+ / Grade A (Refurbished):** Not new. May have minor, often barely visible cosmetic imperfections that do not affect functionality. Seller warranty **1 year** from registration or invoice date.
- Full grade comparison: https://iphonery.com/article/geraete-grade-a-plus-a

## Regional Versions (EU / IN / USA)
For **laptops**: keyboard layout differs by region — check before ordering.

For **iPhones** the main differences are:
1. **USA** — no physical SIM tray (eSIM only). EU and India have nano-SIM + eSIM.
2. **USA iPhone 17 Pro/Pro Max** — slightly larger battery (200–300 mAh more).
3. **mmWave 5G** — only in USA (and partially Japan/Korea). EU/India have Sub-6 GHz 5G only.
4. **Markings** — Indian versions have more regulatory text on back. EU and US are minimal.
5. **All other features** (cameras, display, Apple Intelligence, etc.) are identical across all regions.
6. **IN** — versions have a few more markings/text on the back cover of the phone compared to EU versions.
The main advantage of IN (India) is the noticeably lower price for a brand-new device with exactly the same technical specifications as the EU model

## Warranty
- **New products:** 3-year legal warranty (EU Directive 2019/771). Starts from online registration or invoice date.

- Clients may contact both the manufacturer (e.g. Apple) and Iphonery under warranty.
- Warranty does NOT cover: physical damage, water damage, unauthorised modifications, normal wear, incorrect use, third-party repairs.
- For warranty claims: email info@iphonery.com
- Full details: https://iphonery.com/article/garanty
